# Evaluation Rubric Template

The Interview Coach should score each answer from 1 to 5.

## Clarity

1 = unclear and difficult to follow
3 = understandable but could be clearer
5 = clear, direct, and easy to follow

## Relevance

1 = not connected to the question or job
3 = somewhat relevant but generic
5 = strongly connected to the question and job announcement

## Structure

1 = unstructured or confusing
3 = some structure but not strong
5 = well-structured, for example using Situation-Action-Result or clear beginning-middle-end

## Specificity

1 = very vague
3 = includes some details
5 = includes concrete examples, tools, actions, or outcomes

## Confidence

1 = hesitant, weak, or apologetic
3 = acceptable confidence
5 = confident but not arrogant

## Language Quality

1 = communication is difficult
3 = understandable with some issues
5 = natural, professional, and interview-appropriate

## Important Coaching Rule

The goal is not to create perfect written answers.
The goal is to help the candidate speak better in real interviews.
