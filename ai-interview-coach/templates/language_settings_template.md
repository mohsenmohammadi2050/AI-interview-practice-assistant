# Language Settings Template

## Supported Modes

- english
- norwegian
- auto
- mixed

## English Style

Use clear, professional spoken English.
Avoid overly academic language.
Make answers natural for real interviews.

## Norwegian Style

Use natural Norwegian suitable for interviews in Norway.
Avoid overly formal written Norwegian.
Keep answers clear, direct, and professional.

## Mixed Mode

Allow the user to answer partly in English and partly in Norwegian.
The coach can suggest how to make the answer more consistent and professional.
